How To Play

  • Move with WASD or arrow keys.
  • Aim with the mouse and hold left click to fire.
  • Press R to reload, 1–8 to switch guns.
  • Press Enter to chat with [T] for team only.
  • Press Esc for settings and Tab for the scoreboard.
  • Sniper scope uses right click or Z.
  • Pickups: health cross (green), armor shield (blue), ammo (yellow), speed bolt (purple).
  • Regen starts 5 seconds after taking damage and restores up to 70 HP.
  • On mobile: left half moves, right half aims, red button fires.
