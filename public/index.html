How To Play

  • Move with WASD or arrow keys.
  • Aim with the mouse and hold left click to fire.
  • Press R to reload, Enter to chat, Esc for settings.
  • Sniper scope uses right click or Z.
  • Pickups spawn across the map for health, armor, ammo, and speed.
